The Opportunity

Why China is the GCC's Most Compelling Untapped Destination

China sits at the intersection of ancient grandeur and hyper-modern spectacle — a combination that resonates deeply with Middle East luxury travelers. Yet despite its extraordinary appeal, China remains dramatically under-sold in the GCC market due to operational complexity.

That complexity is exactly where Viluxur steps in.

Challenges Viluxur Solves
Language Barriers
Mandarin-only systems across the entire supplier chain.
Platform Restrictions
WeChat ecosystem required — WhatsApp unavailable throughout.
Payment Complexity
Local payment channels restrict all foreign transactions.
Park Reservations
National parks require pre-booking via local-only systems.
Supplier Access
No direct access to vetted hotels, guides, and operators.
Scale & Distance
A country spanning 5 time zones and vast distances.
Our Story

A Foundation Built Through China

Viluxur built its foundation in the Maldives luxury market, working hand-in-hand with travel agencies across China at the peak of Chinese outbound travel — handling over 3,000 guests per month.

"Through the Maldives, we built relationships that most DMCs spend a decade trying to form. China wasn't just a source market — it became the backbone of our operational DNA."

During COVID-19, our Chinese partners transitioned into domestic inbound tourism, building deep capabilities in logistics, guides, and local supplier networks. Together, we are now bringing that same expertise to inbound China travel — designed specifically for GCC partners.

Curated Programmes

Six Collections. Infinite Possibilities.

Hand-crafted itineraries designed with the GCC traveller in mind — from imperial capitals to Avatar landscapes, from tech megacities to ice festivals.

01

The Classic China

Beijing · Xi'an · Shanghai
The Golden Triangle
Duration: 8–10 Days
Ideal Clients
Families, multi-generation groups, classic luxury travelers
Why It Sells in the Middle East
  • Bucket list landmarks with VIP private tour options
  • Strong luxury hotel inventory throughout
  • Easy direct flight connectivity from GCC
  • High conversion route — clients are already dreaming of it
Key Highlights
The Forbidden City
UNESCO World Heritage imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties — one of the world's great architectural marvels.
Mutianyu Great Wall
One of the finest preserved sections of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall, accessible via private cable car experience.
Terracotta Army, Xi'an
The "Eighth Wonder of the World" — 8,000 warriors buried with China's first Emperor. An unmissable historic encounter.
The Bund, Shanghai
Century-old European architecture facing the hyper-modern Lujiazui skyline across the Huangpu River — iconic day and night.
Shanghai Tower
China's tallest building at 632m — the world's third-tallest — with breathtaking observation deck experiences.

02

Elegant Eastern China

Shanghai · Hangzhou · Suzhou
Refined · Scenic · Slow Luxury
Duration: 6–8 Days
Ideal Clients
Honeymooners, ladies groups, premium FIT travelers
Why It Sells in the Middle East
  • Nature + soft culture — unhurried and deeply aesthetic
  • Exceptionally Instagram-worthy at every turn
  • Luxury boutique experiences at every stop
  • Less hectic than Beijing — ideal for refined clientele
Key Highlights
West Lake, Hangzhou
One of China's most poetic landscapes — ten legendary scenes that have inspired Chinese art and literature for millennia.
The Humble Administrator's Garden
Suzhou's masterpiece classical garden — China's largest, dividing into three sections of extraordinary landscape artistry.
Zhouzhuang Water Town
China's most celebrated water town — Ming and Qing residences crisscrossed by ancient waterways and twin stone bridges.
Lingyin Temple
A 1,700-year-old temple nestled among ancient trees and Feilai Peak grottoes — one of Buddhism's most revered sites.
Huangpu River Night Cruise
Experience the dual personality of Shanghai by night — historic Bund on one side, neon-lit Pudong skyline on the other.

03

Scenic WoW China

Beijing · Zhangjiajie · Shanghai
Dramatic Landscapes · Social Media China
Duration: 8–10 Days
Ideal Clients
Young professionals, adventure-seeking families, social media-conscious travelers
Why It's Trending
  • Avatar mountains — extraordinary visual appeal unlike anywhere else
  • Glass bridge experiences that create extraordinary moments
  • Maximum social media impact — content gold for every client
  • Blends iconic Beijing culture with untamed natural drama
Key Highlights
Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
UNESCO World Natural Heritage Site — the real-life inspiration for the floating "Avatar" mountains. Breathtaking at every angle.
Tianmen Mountain
"The Soul of Zhangjiajie" — features the world's longest alpine cableway and the dramatic Tianmen Cave opening in the cliff face.
Glass Bridge (Yuntiandu)
One of the world's highest and longest glass-bottomed bridges — a test of courage with unparalleled canyon views below.
Huanglong Cave
A "World Karst Wonder" — a vast underground palace of formations spanning 15km, among China's most spectacular natural caves.
Baofeng Lake
Crystal-clear waters nestled among towering peaks — a serene and mirror-like landscape of extraordinary natural beauty.

04

Modern South China

Guangzhou · Shenzhen · Hong Kong
Shopping · Business · Contemporary China
Duration: 6–8 Days
Ideal Clients
Repeat China travelers, business families, shopping enthusiasts
Why Middle East Clients Choose It
  • Heavy shopping focus — fashion, tech, and wholesale markets
  • Modern city vibe — less historical, more lifestyle and energy
  • Halal dining widely available in Hong Kong
  • Comfortable transition for more conservative GCC travelers
Key Highlights
Victoria Harbour, HK
The iconic heart of Hong Kong — the "Symphony of Lights" show and the legendary Star Ferry crossing the world-famous harbour.
Canton Tower, Guangzhou
China's tallest tower featuring the world's highest Ferris wheel and a sky drop thrill ride at 488 metres.
Pearl River Night Cruise
Guangzhou's dazzling nocturnal skyline — illuminated landmarks reflecting across the river in a spectacular night panorama.
Chimelong Resort
World-class resort complex including one of Asia's largest safari parks, major amusement rides, and an acclaimed water park.
Ping An Finance Centre, Shenzhen
Shenzhen's tallest tower at 547.6m — the 116th-floor Sky Observation Deck offers 360° views across Shenzhen, Hong Kong, and beyond.

05

Winter Magic Edition

Beijing · Harbin Ice Festival
Seasonal Luxury · January – February Only
Duration: 8–10 Days
Ideal Clients
GCC winter novelty seekers, families wanting snow experiences
Why It Attracts Middle East Travelers
  • Snow novelty — many GCC clients have never experienced a true winter
  • Ice castles and frozen sculpture worlds unlike anywhere on earth
  • Unique, emotionally charged experiences unavailable in the Gulf
  • Exclusive seasonal window creates urgency and desirability
Key Highlights
Harbin Ice & Snow World
The world's premier ice theme park — giant ice castles, record-breaking ice slides, and luminous snow performances built from the frozen Songhua River.
Siberian Tiger Park
The world's largest Siberian tiger breeding base — a rare opportunity to observe these magnificent creatures in a near-wild environment.
Sun Island Snow Expo
Giant snow sculptures on an island setting — a winter wonderland that complements Harbin's famous ice city perfectly.
Saint Sophia Cathedral
A stunning Byzantine-style Russian Orthodox cathedral — an architectural anomaly in the heart of Chinese Manchuria, beautiful under snow.
Beijing Imperial Highlights
The Forbidden City, Summer Palace, and Great Wall in their winter splendour — a dramatically different and unforgettable visual experience.

06

Mainland + Hong Kong + Macau

Beijing · Shanghai · Hong Kong · Macau
China Icons + International Lifestyle
Duration: 10–12 Days
Ideal Clients
Premium families, honeymooners, VIP FIT travelers
Why It Sells So Well in the Middle East
  • Combines cultural depth with a familiar international lifestyle feel
  • Hong Kong and Macau offer comfort for first-time China travelers
  • Halal food availability makes it ideal for conservative GCC clients
  • Excellent shopping across all segments — luxury, fashion, wholesale
Key Highlights
Beijing Imperial Circuit
The Forbidden City, Summer Palace, and Mutianyu Great Wall — a private, unhurried journey through China's imperial heart.
Shanghai Tower & The Bund
Old and new Shanghai in a single view — the century-old Bund waterfront facing the soaring glass towers of Lujiazui.
Victoria Peak, Hong Kong
Ride the historic Peak Tram to the summit for iconic panoramic views of Victoria Harbour — one of the world's great urban vistas.
Macau's Grand Glamour
Baroque Portuguese architecture alongside Asia's entertainment capital — a unique blend of European heritage and spectacular modernity.
Hong Kong Disneyland
A magical world of fairy tales set against Hong Kong's dramatic backdrop — perfect for families seeking unforgettable moments together.
